# HeyUp — iPhone screenshot plan

Use six portrait screenshots from the largest supported iPhone display. Keep the real interface visible and use short, high-contrast captions in the HeyUp dark-and-lime style.

1. **Turn screen time into movement time**
   - Screen: Home
   - Show the interval, session length, and exercise choice.

2. **Friendly reminders, on your schedule**
   - Screen: Active timer
   - Show a realistic countdown and pause controls.

3. **Your quick movement break**
   - Screen: Get Ready
   - Show the chosen exercise and clear preparation guidance.

4. **Private, on-device rep counting**
   - Screen: Movement Break
   - Show the camera overlay and a mid-session rep count.
   - Use a consenting adult model and avoid showing private surroundings.

5. **Small wins add up**
   - Screen: Success
   - Show a completed break and streak message.

6. **See your momentum grow**
   - Screen: History
   - Seed realistic but honest demonstration data before capture.

## Capture rules

- Capture from the release build, not a design mockup.
- Do not show debug overlays, personal notifications, or status-bar information that should remain private.
- Keep all claims consistent with what version 1.0 actually does.
- Avoid medical, weight-loss, or guaranteed-outcome claims.
- Verify exact screenshot dimensions against App Store Connect when the app record is created.
