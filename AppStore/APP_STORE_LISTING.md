## Promotional text

Turn screen time into movement time with friendly reminders, quick exercise breaks, and private on-device rep counting.

## Description

HeyUp helps you break up long stretches of screen time with short, achievable movement breaks.

Choose how often you want a reminder, how long you plan to settle in, and the exercise that suits you. When it is time to move, HeyUp guides you through a quick break and uses your iPhone camera to count each rep as you go.

FEATURES

• Flexible reminders for TV time, home-office sessions, and daily life
• Squats, seated squats, wall push-ups, knee push-ups, and floor push-ups
• Mix and combination modes for more variety
• Live rep counting using on-device body-pose detection
• Daily progress, exercise history, and streaks
• Gentle starting recommendations based on your preferences
• No account, advertising, or cloud upload required

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

Camera frames are analyzed live on your iPhone. HeyUp does not record, save, or upload camera footage. Your settings and progress stay on your device.

HeyUp is a general wellness and movement-reminder app. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Stop exercising if you feel pain or unwell.

## App Review notes

HeyUp requests notification permission during onboarding to schedule local movement reminders. It requests camera permission before the first camera-counted movement break.

To reach the camera feature:

1. Complete onboarding.
2. On Home, select a reminder interval and tap Start.
3. On the timer screen, tap the option to begin a break now rather than waiting for the timer.
4. Continue through Get Ready to open the movement-break camera.

The front-camera feed is processed on-device with Apple Vision body-pose detection. Camera frames are not recorded, stored, transmitted, or shared. The app has no account or login requirement.

Testing rep counting requires a physical iPhone and enough space for the relevant joints to be visible.
